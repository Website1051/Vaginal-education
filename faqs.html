Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about vaginal health.

1. How often should I clean my vaginal area?

It is recommended to clean the vaginal area daily with warm water. Avoid using harsh soaps or douching as it can disrupt the natural balance.

2. Is vaginal discharge normal?

Yes, vaginal discharge is normal and helps to keep the area clean. However, changes in color, smell, or consistency may indicate an infection.

3. What can I do about painful periods?

To ease period pain, you can use a heating pad, take over-the-counter pain relievers, and stay hydrated. If pain is severe, consult a doctor.

4. How can I prevent urinary tract infections (UTIs)?

Drink plenty of water, urinate after intercourse, wipe from front to back, and avoid using irritating feminine products.

5. How often should I see a gynecologist?

It is recommended to visit a gynecologist at least once a year for a routine check-up and more frequently if you experience any issues.
