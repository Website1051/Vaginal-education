How to Maintain Good Vaginal Health

Follow these steps to ensure proper vaginal hygiene and overall well-being.

1. Maintain Proper Hygiene

Use mild soap and warm water to clean the vaginal area. Avoid douching and harsh products.

2. Wear Breathable Cotton Underwear

Choose cotton underwear to reduce moisture buildup and prevent infections.

3. Eat a Healthy Diet

Incorporate probiotics (e.g., yogurt), stay hydrated, and reduce sugar intake.

4. Practice Safe Sex

Use protection and get regular STI screenings to maintain reproductive health.
